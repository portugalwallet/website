Privacy Policy

How The Portugal Wallet collects, uses and protects your data.

Last updated: April 2025

Who we are

The Portugal Wallet (theportugalwallet.com) is an independent financial information website. For data protection purposes, we are the data controller.

Contact: privacy@theportugalwallet.com

What data we collect

Newsletter subscribers (via Kit)

  • Email address
  • Date and time of signup
  • Which page you signed up from
  • Email open and click data (managed by Kit)

Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
Retention: Until you unsubscribe

Website analytics (with your consent)

  • Pages visited, time on page, referral source
  • Device type and browser (anonymised)
  • Country-level location (no precise location)

Legal basis: Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
Retention: 26 months (Google Analytics default)

What we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, addresses or phone numbers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Precise location data
  • Any data from children under 16
  • Data for advertising or marketing profiling

Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

Essential cookies: Required for the site to work (e.g. remembering your cookie preference). These are set regardless of your choice.

Analytics cookies: Used to understand how our guides are used so we can improve them. These are only set if you click Accept on our cookie banner.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Kit (newsletter): kit.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics (site analytics — only with consent): policies.google.com/privacy
  • GitHub Pages (website hosting): docs.github.com/en/site-policy

Your rights under GDPR

As an EU resident you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ('right to be forgotten')
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority (in Portugal: CNPD — cnpd.pt)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at: privacy@theportugalwallet.com
We will respond within 30 days.

Newsletter unsubscribe

Every newsletter email contains a one-click unsubscribe link at the bottom. Clicking it immediately removes you from our list. We do not ask why.

Changes to this policy

We will notify newsletter subscribers of any material changes to this policy. The date at the top of this page always shows when it was last updated.
